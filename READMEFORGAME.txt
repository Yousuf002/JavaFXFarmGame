On enter on feed picks up animal's feed.
There are 4 cows and 4 animals.
After picking up chicken's feed pressing enter on any of the chicken feeds the chicken.
Same goes for cow.
On feeding chickens and cows both once in given time will increase the level.
If failed to eat chicken in given time it will decrease the level.
if you are on level 1 and fail to feed chicken and cow once it will be a GAME OVER!

If both feeds are picked without feeding animals, you can feed any one of the animal, after that you have to pick other feed again to feed the other animal it will drop the feed of the animal that is not fed.
